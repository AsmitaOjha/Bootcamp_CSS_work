Manoj Shukla is an Indian Lyricist, poet, and screenwriter.He has penned down the Bollywood songs such as Galliyan, Tere Sang Yaara, Kaun Tujhe,and Teri Mitti. A book of Sahir Ludhianvi inspired him to take up his interest in writing poems as a profession. He changed his surname to 'Muntashir', which means 'a scattered soul'. He took the name because no poet ever used that name and it was unique to him.
